# Order Management FAQs

    ## General Questions

    ### How do I place an order?
    You can place an order by using the "createOrder" tool with your name, items, and shipping address.

    ### How can I check my order status?
    Use the "viewOrder" tool with your order ID to check the current status of your order.

    ### Can I cancel my order?
    Yes, you can cancel your order by using the "deleteOrder" tool with your order ID, as long as the order hasn't shipped yet.

    ## Order Processing

    ### How long does order processing take?
    Order processing typically takes 1-2 business days before shipping.

    ### What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

    ### Do you offer international shipping?
    Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping may take 7-14 business days.

    ## Returns and Refunds

    ### What is your return policy?
    Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

    ### How do I initiate a return?
    Contact customer service with your order ID to initiate the return process.

    ### How long do refunds take to process?
    Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
